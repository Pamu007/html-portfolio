My top 5 movies of all-time

Arguably the best movie trilogy of all-time? It's a one of a kind movie for the fantasy lovers and the CGI is dope!

Titanic

As advertised, the hype is real!!! The movie is emotioal and it's a must-watch!

Avatar

One of the greatest animated movies ever. Amazing concept and captivating story.

Harry Potter movie series

Childhood favourite! Any age group can enjoy this without hesitation!

Saving Private Ryan

If you like war/action movies, this is my recommendation!
